About Us

As mentioned, we give speeches of under one minute to the students of Renaissance High School. In the future, we intend to expand to other schools and places. The SoapBox Intention is a student run and contributed group who's goal is to share ideas they fell are important or interesting, with the end goal of pushing the student body to interesting palces of action.
